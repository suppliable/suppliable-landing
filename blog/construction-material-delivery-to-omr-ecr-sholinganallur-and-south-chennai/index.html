Construction Material Delivery to OMR, ECR, Sholinganallur and South Chennai

12 April 2026 · 5 min read ·by Suppliable team

Suppliable operates a 60-minute delivery service for construction materials across South Chennai. We're based in Sholinganallur and most of our orders go to sites on OMR, ECR, and the corridor between them — which makes sense given South Chennai is where most new residential and commercial construction is happening.

Here's the working coverage, typical delivery times, and how it all works.

Coverage map (May 2026)

If your construction site is in one of these areas, we'll deliver in 60 minutes from order confirmation during business hours (7 AM – 8 PM):

  • Thoraipakkam
  • Perungudi
  • Karapakkam
  • OMR — between Tidel Park and Siruseri
  • ECR — between Akkarai and Muttukadu
  • Navalur

  • OMR — Tidel Park to Adyar end

  • ECR — Muttukadu to Mahabalipuram

On-request zone — same-day delivery

We deliver but timing varies based on traffic and order size:

  • Tambaram (West) / Chromepet
  • Mylapore / Triplicane

If you're in any other Chennai area, WhatsApp us — we can usually arrange same-day or next-morning delivery to anywhere within Chennai district.

Why South Chennai (and where we go next)

Chennai's south side has the highest density of active construction — residential apartments along OMR, villas on ECR, commercial development around Tidel Park / Sholinganallur tech corridor. That's where our 60-minute promise actually holds.

Outside this base, traffic is the main variable. Anywhere on the West Tambaram side adds 30-60 minutes during peak hours.

Coming soon: we're piloting hubs in Porur (West Chennai) and Tambaram (South-West) for late 2026 to bring 60-min delivery to those zones too. If you have regular projects in those areas, tell us — high contractor demand accelerates the rollout.

How orders work

Three ways to order:

  1. Suppliable app — Available on Android (iOS coming). Browse, search, add to cart, set delivery address, pay. Track delivery live like a Swiggy/Zomato order. Download here →

  2. WhatsApp — message +91 87786 27926 with what you need. Our team responds in 15-20 minutes with a quote, and dispatches as soon as you confirm.

  3. Bulk quote form — for large or complex BOMs, use the bulk quote form on our homepage. We'll email a detailed quote with line items, taxes, delivery, payment terms.

Delivery vehicles and capacity

We use a mix:

  • Small trucks (Tata Ace / Mahindra Bolero Pickup) — for orders up to 1 tonne. Fits 25-30 cement bags, 100-200 bricks, or assorted small items.
  • Mid-size trucks (Tata 407 / Eicher Pro 1049) — for orders up to 3 tonnes. Standard truckload of cement (60 bags) or AAC blocks.
  • Larger trucks (12-tonne / tipper) — for sand, aggregates, large block orders.

Cement and dry materials are covered with tarp regardless of weather.

Payment options

  • UPI — instant, no fees, fastest. PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, BHIM — all work.
  • Bank transfer / RTGS — for bulk orders, payment terms negotiable.
  • Cash on delivery — for small orders only, by prior arrangement.
  • Credit terms — available for regular contractors after KYC. Ask us.

Returns and damage

  • Damaged-in-transit goods are replaced free or refunded — report within 2 hours of delivery with a photo.
  • Wrong-spec items sent (our error) are exchanged free, fastest delivery slot.
  • Unused materials that are in original packaging — returns accepted within 24 hours, 10% restocking fee for opened categories.

Why South Chennai needs this

The construction boom along OMR, ECR, and the broader Sholinganallur–Mahabalipuram corridor has outgrown the traditional bulk-yard model. Sites are smaller (individual villas, mid-rise apartments), turn faster, and need materials in smaller, more frequent batches — not 5-tonne truckloads delivered once.

That's the niche Suppliable fills: app-ordered, same-hour delivery, no minimum quantities for most items. Built for the kind of construction actually happening in Chennai today.

If you're running a site anywhere in our coverage map, give us a try on your next material order. The first delivery is usually how contractors realise how much easier this is than their existing supply chain.
