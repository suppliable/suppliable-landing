Why we built a native app instead of doing this on the web

Suppliable.in has worked since day one — you can browse the catalogue, calculate materials, and WhatsApp a bulk quote from any browser. So why an app?

Three reasons, all from listening to Chennai contractors over the last six months:

  1. One-tap reorder. Contractors order the same SKUs over and over — UltraTech PPC 50 kg, Ramco Block Fix 40 kg, Asian Paints Tractor 20 L. A native app puts those one tap away from a phone's home screen. The web makes you sign in, navigate, type.

  2. Push notifications for delivery. When your slab pour starts at 6 AM and the delivery is 45 minutes out, you want a notification, not an SMS lost in the spam folder. Native push is reliable; SMS in India isn't.

  3. Offline cart. Construction sites in OMR and ECR have patchy mobile signal. A native app lets you build a cart offline, then send it when signal returns. Web doesn't.

We're keeping suppliable.in for first-time discovery, calculators, and SEO. The app is for repeat orders and active site days.

Frequently asked questions

Is the Suppliable app free?

Yes — the app is free to download from the Google Play Store. You only pay for what you order.

Which cities does Suppliable cover?

Chennai and surrounding areas. We're actively serving OMR, ECR, Velachery, Adyar, Pallavaram, Tambaram and South Chennai with 60-minute delivery from our Sholinganallur warehouse. WhatsApp +91 87786 27926 to check if we cover your specific location.

Do I need a GST number to use Suppliable?

No. Personal accounts work with just a phone number. GST is required only if you want a tax invoice for input-tax-credit claims.

Can I order without installing the app?

Yes — order through suppliable.in or WhatsApp +91 87786 27926. The app is optional but faster for repeat orders.

What's the minimum order?

Most items have no minimum. TMT steel needs 500 kg minimum per diameter for direct site delivery. Loose sand and aggregate (sand and gravel page) is sold by the tonne.

Is there an iOS app?

The Android app is live now. iOS is on the roadmap for later this year.

What if I'm not happy with what's delivered?

We have a strict refund policy — wrong items, damaged sealed packs, or quantity shortages are refundable if flagged within 4 hours of delivery, on the challan. Change-of-mind returns are not accepted because construction materials can't be resold once delivered.
