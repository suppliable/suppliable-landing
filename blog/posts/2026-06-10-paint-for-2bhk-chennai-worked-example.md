## The 2BHK we're estimating

Standard Chennai 2BHK floor plan:

| Room | Floor area | Wall area (×3.5) |
|---|---|---|
| Living + dining | 350 sqft | 1,000 sqft |
| Master bedroom | 150 sqft | 480 sqft |
| Bedroom 2 | 120 sqft | 380 sqft |
| Kitchen | 100 sqft | 320 sqft |
| Bathroom × 2 | 80 sqft | 250 sqft |
| Circulation | 100 sqft | 320 sqft |
| **Total** | **900 sqft** | **~2,750 sqft** |

Subtract doors (~5 × 21 = 105 sqft) and windows (~8 × 12 = 96 sqft): **net 2,550 sqft of interior wall**.

For exteriors, assume 1,800 sqft of external wall on a ground-floor independent house. (For an apartment, skip the exterior — that's the builder's problem.)

## Interior paint — Asian Paints Tractor Emulsion

The workhorse interior paint for value Chennai builds. Official coverage: **140 sqft per litre per coat**. 2 coats standard.

```
Litres = (2,550 sqft × 2 coats) ÷ 140 sqft/L
       = 5,100 ÷ 140
       = 36.4 L
```

Round up to **40 L**: one 20 L bucket + two 10 L cans. At Suppliable's live price of ~₹190/L (1 L pricing; cheaper per litre at bucket sizes), that's roughly **₹6,500** in interior paint.

If you want the premium feel in just the living room, swap to **Royale Luxury Emulsion** for that one room — costs about ₹3,000 more for 8 litres but gives the washable smooth finish that scrubs clean.

## Exterior paint — Asian Paints Ace Exterior Emulsion

For the external walls. Official coverage: **110 sqft per litre per coat**. 2 coats standard.

```
Litres = (1,800 × 2) ÷ 110
       = 3,600 ÷ 110
       = 32.7 L
```

Round up to **35 L**: one 20 L bucket + one 10 L can + one 4 L can. At ~₹250/L, that's ~**₹8,750** in exterior paint.

## Primer

You need primer under both interior and exterior paint. Wall primer covers about 140 sqft/L (1 coat). Total wall area = 2,550 + 1,800 = 4,350 sqft.

```
Primer litres = 4,350 ÷ 140 = 31 L
```

Round to **35 L** for the priming pass. At ~₹220/L, that's ~**₹7,700**.

## Wall putty

Coverage: ~50 sqft per kg for 2 thin coats. For interior walls only (you'd skip on exterior unless it's badly damaged plaster):

```
Putty kg = 2,550 ÷ 50 = 51 kg
```

Round to **60 kg**: 3 bags of Asian Paints TruCare Wall Putty 20 kg. At ~₹620 per bag, that's **₹1,860**.

## Common mistakes that wreck the estimate

1. **Forgetting to multiply by coats.** People estimate single-coat coverage. Triples the under-order risk.
2. **Using exterior coverage for interior or vice-versa.** Tractor's 140 sqft/L is not Ace's 110 sqft/L.
3. **Counting only floor area instead of wall area.** Wall area = ~3.5 × floor area for standard 10 ft ceiling rooms.
4. **Skipping primer.** Primer at ₹220/L is cheaper than the repainting you'll do in 18 months without it.
