Frequently asked

Is Suppliable a hardware shop or a building materials store?

Both. We carry the full hardware + construction materials catalogue — small fittings like fasteners, drill bits and binding wire, all the way up to tonnes of cement and TMT steel. We're more of a "one-stop construction site supplier" than a traditional retail hardware shop.

Do you sell sand and gravel?

Yes — M-sand, P-sand, river sand, and 6 mm to 40 mm jelly (aggregate). Loose materials are quoted by the tonne and delivered by tipper. See our Sand & Gravel page or WhatsApp the requirement to +91 87786 27926.

What's the minimum order?

Most items have no minimum. TMT steel needs 500 kg minimum per diameter for direct site delivery. Loose sand and aggregate is sold by the tonne.

Do you take cash on delivery?

Yes — for orders under ₹10,000. Larger orders need UPI / card / bank transfer at the time of order confirmation.

Can I visit the warehouse?

Yes — 98 Kalaignar Karunanidhi Salai, Sholinganallur, Chennai 600119. Mon-Sat, 9 AM – 7 PM. But the fastest way to order is WhatsApp — we'll have the material at your site in an hour without you leaving the construction.
