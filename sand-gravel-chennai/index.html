How much sand and gravel do I need?

For typical Chennai construction work, rule-of-thumb quantities per 1000 sqft:

  • RCC roof slab (125 mm thick, M20): ~180 cft (~8 tonnes) M-sand + ~360 cft (~16 tonnes) 20 mm jelly
  • Plastering (12 mm internal, both sides): ~50 cft (~2.3 tonnes) P-sand
  • Block masonry (6-inch AAC, 1500 sqft wall): minimal — block fix adhesive replaces mortar
  • Brick masonry (1000 sqft wall): ~30 cft (~1.4 tonnes) M-sand

Why "near me" matters for sand and gravel

Sand and aggregate are heavy and bulk — a tonne occupies roughly 0.7 m³. Transport cost from quarry to site is half the delivered price. Searching "sand near me" in Chennai is the right instinct: the closer your supplier's warehouse to your site, the lower the per-tonne delivered cost. Quality and source

All sand is screened and washed (where applicable) at the quarry before loading. We stock from approved Tamil Nadu quarries with valid TN PWD / TWAD certification. River sand availability is seasonal and regulated — when river sand is scarce, M-sand is the recommended substitute (M-sand is technically superior for most RCC applications and is what most Chennai builders use by default in 2026).
