💧 Dr. Fixit Waterproofing Calculator

Pick the application — terrace, bathroom, water tank, external wall or LW+ dosage — and get the exact Dr. Fixit product and quantity for the job.

Terrace — Dr. Fixit Newcoat

Acrylic terrace waterproofing. Apply 2 coats. Official coverage: 22 sqft/L per coat.

Bathroom — Dr. Fixit Bathseal

Cement-based, applied to floor + walls (up to 1 ft above floor for wet area). Official coverage: 6.5 sqft/kg (2 coats).

Water Tank — Dr. Fixit Pidifin 2K

2-component cementitious coating. Applied to inside of RCC tanks (all 4 walls + floor). Official coverage: 1.5 kg/m² (2 coats) — one 15 kg kit covers ~108 sqft.

External Wall — Dr. Fixit Raincoat

Elastomeric exterior wall coating. Typically 2 coats. Official coverage: 86 sqft/L per coat (8 sqm/L).

LW+ Dosage — Dr. Fixit Pidiproof LW+

Integral waterproofing admixture for RCC. 200 ml per 50 kg cement bag.
