Why a Chennai-specific calculator?

Construction material pricing varies 10-20% between Indian cities. Cement is cheaper in Tamil Nadu than in Mumbai because Ramco, Chettinad, Dalmia and India Cements all run nearby plants. TMT steel from ARS Chennai costs ₹2-3 less per kg than the same grade delivered to Bangalore. Asian Paints Tractor sells at the same MRP nationally, but the dealer margin and Suppliable's contractor-bulk pricing is what makes Chennai numbers different.

The Suppliable Material Calculator pulls live unit prices from our Chennai catalogue — what we'd actually quote you today for delivery in Sholinganallur, OMR, ECR or anywhere in South Chennai. The math (D²/162 for steel, 1.54 dry-volume factor for concrete, official Dr. Fixit coverage rates) is the same anywhere in India, but the cost numbers below them are Chennai-specific.
