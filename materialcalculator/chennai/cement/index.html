Three calculations, one tool

The Suppliable Cement Calculator covers the three jobs Chennai contractors estimate most often:

  • Plastering — wall area × thickness × mix ratio (1:4 internal, 1:6 external). Returns bags of cement + cft of sand.
  • Columns & beams — L × W × H × grade (M20/M25/M30). Returns bags + sand + aggregate.
  • RCC roof slab — L × W × thickness × grade. Same outputs, sized for slab pours.

All three use the 1.54 dry-volume factor and Indian standard mix ratios. For a worked example, see How Many Cement Bags for a 1000 sqft RCC Slab.

Which brand for which job

  • UltraTech — premium choice for visible structural members (columns, beams). Highest brand recall.
  • Ramco — Chennai-headquartered, most reliable supply, our most-ordered for general construction.
  • Dalmia — strong on plastering and finishing jobs.
  • India Cements — cost-conscious option for non-structural work like compound walls, sub-base.
