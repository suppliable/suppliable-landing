Chennai monsoon timing — when to waterproof

Chennai's northeast monsoon hits in October-November. Best windows for waterproofing work:

  • July-August — ideal terrace + exterior wall season. Dry surface, 3 months for full cure before rains.
  • December-February — second-best window after monsoon ends.
  • March-May — too hot for some coatings; check product temperature limits.
  • September — too risky, rain can interrupt cure cycle.

See our Chennai monsoon waterproofing checklist for the full pre-rain prep guide.

Why Chennai needs more waterproofing than most cities

800-1200 mm of rain in 6 weeks during the northeast monsoon, plus year-round salt-laden coastal air east of OMR, plus 35°C+ summer heat that opens micro-cracks in RCC. Skipping integral waterproofing (LW+) at pour time and topical coating (Newcoat) post-pour means leaks by year 2-3. The calculator helps you size both correctly so the slab survives 8-10 years before re-coat.
